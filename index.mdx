## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at the MotionOps dashboard or accept an invitation from your organization admin. Verify your email to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a camera">
    Add a camera with an RTSP or HTTPS stream URL. MotionOps validates the source, connects the AI worker, and begins streaming within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor events">
    Events appear in real time on the dashboard. Review detections, adjust confidence thresholds, and export your audit trail any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
