MotionOps connects your IP cameras and RTSP streams to an AI detection pipeline that surfaces real-time security events, organizes them into a reviewable trail, and logs every action your team takes — all through a unified dashboard or REST API. Whether you're monitoring a single site or managing a multi-camera deployment across locations, MotionOps gives you the visibility and controls to stay on top of what's happening.

## What MotionOps does

**Live camera feeds** — Stream RTSP and RTSPS video in real time. MotionOps validates your source URL, connects an AI worker, and starts delivering frames within seconds of adding a camera.

**AI motion detection** — A YOLO-based detection engine continuously analyzes each feed for people, vehicles, and custom objects. You configure confidence thresholds and detection zones; MotionOps does the rest.

**Event trail** — Every detection becomes a reviewable event with a severity level (`info` through `critical`), a snapshot, and full metadata. Operators and analysts triage events directly in the dashboard or via the API.

**Audit log** — Every action — camera added, event reviewed, user role changed — is written to a tamper-evident audit log capturing the actor, timestamp, IP address, and before/after values. The log is exportable for compliance reporting.

**Role-based access** — Five built-in roles (Viewer, Operator, Analyst, Admin, Super Admin) let you give each team member exactly the permissions they need. Admins can invite users and adjust roles without touching the API.
